What Stands Out

Counter takes four of the top five spots. That is not because of any single standout product. It is because every item in their lineup lands between 0.084 and 0.100 on ratio. The consistency across the whole line is what separates them. You can grab any Counter box at Target without reading the label and know you are getting 30-31g of protein in 310-370 calories.

Healthy Choice's Basil Pesto Chicken is a sleeper pick at 0.095, but most of their other Power Bowls sit in the 0.070s. Vital Pursuit is solid across the board and about a dollar cheaper per meal. Lean Cuisine and Kevin's both have individual products worth buying, but neither line is consistent enough to recommend broadly.
